Client

Ukiyo is an online retailer of Japanese-inspired home goods. They offer a variety of products, including candles, incense, and other decorative items.

Challenge

Ukiyo wanted to rebrand their website to create a more cohesive and visually appealing experience for their customers. They also wanted to make their website more competitive with other online retailers.

Solution

I worked with Ukiyosense.com to rebrand their website from the ground up. I started by creating a new brand identity that was inspired by the Japanese aesthetic. I then created a new design for the website that was based on the new brand identity. The new website features a clean and modern design that is both visually appealing and easy to use. The website also uses high-quality photography and video to showcase the products.
