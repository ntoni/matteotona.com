Client

LexDo.it is a legal contracts automation platform. They offer a variety of services to businesses and individuals,including company formation, contract drafting, and legal advice.

Challenge

Lexdo wanted to improve the user experience of its platform in order to attract new customers and retain existing ones. The platform was originally designed with a focus on functionality, but it lacked some of the usability and visual appeal that users expect from modern software.

Solution

We worked with Lexdo to develop a comprehensive UX redesign. The redesign focused on the following areas: Usability: We made the platform easier to use by simplifying the navigation, improving the labeling, and providing more helpful error messages. Visual appeal: We redesigned the platform's interface to be more modern and appealing, using a consistent color scheme and font.

Results

The UX redesign was a success. We received positive feedback from users, who reported that the platform was easier to use and more visually appealing. We also saw a significant increase in the number of new customers signing up for the platform.

Conclusion

You worked with Lexdo to develop a design system that would be used across all of their products. This design system helped to create a consistent user experience across the platform. You conducted user research to understand the needs of Lexdo's users. This research helped you to identify the areas where the platform could be improved. You created a variety of prototypes and wireframes to test different design ideas. This iterative process helped you to develop a design that was both functional and user-friendly.
